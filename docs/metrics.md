## Source of truth

Declared metrics are defined in:

- `apps/api/src/observability/api-metrics.ts`
- `libs/chain/src/metrics/metrics.ts`
- `apps/indexer/src/derivation/derivation-metrics.ts`
- `apps/indexer/src/derivation/calldata-decode-metrics.ts`
- `nest/sources/aave/src/aave-metrics.ts`
- `nest/sources/reconcile-metrics.ts`

Notes:

- Counter names are declared without `_total`; Prometheus exposition appends `_total`.
- `indexer_ingestion_dlq_size` is emitted under the `indexer_ingestion_*` family (not a separate emitted `dlq_*` family in M1).
- Metric names are prefixed by sanitized `OTEL_SERVICE_NAME` (for example `api`, `indexer`, `ai_worker`).
- `indexer_state_reconcile_*` is shared by Compound and Aave; distinguish sources via the `source_type` label.
- `indexer_state_reconcile_*` now covers `aave_governance_v3_reconcile` and `aave_payloads_controller_reconcile` in addition to Compound reconciler source types.
- `indexer_aave_vote_derivation_total` uses `outcome` values `derived`, `skipped_idempotent`, `failed`, `held`, and `noop`; `held` is the indefinite `no_proposal` retry path and `noop` is the terminal no-op derive for `ProposalResultsSent` and `ProposalVoteConfigurationBridged`.
- `indexer_stitch_pending_seconds` records the oldest held Aave stitch age per voting chain and event type; alert on backlog age rather than retry count.
- `indexer_stitch_payload_pending_seconds` records the oldest held Aave payload stitch age per destination chain and event type.
- `indexer_stitch_unmatched_payload` records the latest batch-scoped held-count for payload-controller events that arrived before their declared payload rows.
