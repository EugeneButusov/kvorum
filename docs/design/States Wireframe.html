State primitives are reusable across all surfaces Block-level errors > full-page errors · stale > silent · always show provenance kvorum.xyz
States · cross-cutting matrix. Three rules: errors are block-level when possible (keep the rest of the page alive), stale beats silent (always show how fresh the data is), and AI panels never fake confidence (low-confidence = redact, not hallucinate).
