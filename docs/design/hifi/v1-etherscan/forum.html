Summary: we propose raising the kink point on the cETH IR curve from 90% to 92% and increasing the jump multiplier from 3.5 to 4.0. Goal: incentivize utilization at the upper band where ETH borrowing demand is currently capped by suboptimal rate ceilings.

Parameter sweep — see cETH_ir_curve_v3.csv attached. Headline: at 95% utilization, borrow APR moves from 11.2% → 13.8%; supply APR moves 7.4% → 8.9%. We model a +18% net protocol revenue at current utilization patterns over 90 days.

Risks: cETH borrow caps remain at 78k; if utilization sustains above the new kink, cap exhaustion accelerates. Mitigations modeled in §3 of the parameter sweep.

Snapshot signal targets ≥75% support before onchain. Voting is open at snapshot.org/#/comp.eth/proposal/0x4f....

Appreciate the detailed sweep. We have a concern with the borrow-cap interaction at the new kink. The model in §3 assumes utilization plateaus at 95% — but our backtest of analogous markets shows a 2.4× cap-exhaustion frequency within 30 days when curves shift this aggressively.

Recommendation: kink at 91% (not 92%) for one cycle; revisit in 30 days with cap utilization data. We're voting Against in current form. Happy to coordinate with the Gauntlet team on a v2 parameter set.
