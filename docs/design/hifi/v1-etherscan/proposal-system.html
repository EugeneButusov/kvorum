02Description

source of truth · markdown · 3,212 words

Update interest-rate curve, cETH market

This proposal updates the interest-rate model on the cETH market to better respond to high-utilization periods. Recent observations show that during stress events, utilization climbed past 90% with insufficient rate response, leading to thin available liquidity for redemptions.

The proposed change lowers the kink point from 80% to 70% and increases the jump multiplier so that borrow rates rise more steeply once the kink is crossed. This is a modest tweak to align with parameters used in similar markets across the protocol.

No other parameters of the cETH market are affected by this proposal. Risk has been reviewed by Gauntlet and the Risk WG, with simulated outcomes attached to the linked forum thread.

If accepted, the new model is deployed at 0x7f8c…91ab (JumpRateModelV2) with the parameters above. Existing borrowers will see borrow rates adjust at the next interest accrual after execution.
