05Governance tracks

single-track DAO
Structure
Compound runs a single onchain track: COMP-weighted votes through Governor Bravo, executed behind a 2-day timelock. There are no parallel governance venues to reconcile. Multi-track DAOs (Lido — Aragon, Snapshot, Easy Track, Dual Governance) surface each track as a separate panel here; for Compound the whole picture lives on one governor.
