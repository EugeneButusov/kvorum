Reflow rules

What the rest of the dashboard does at <720pt. Desktop-first pages still load — they just don't get bespoke treatment.

≥1024
Desktop. Full topnav, sidebars, two-column layouts as designed.
920–1023
Tablet wide. Sidebars collapse into anchored sub-nav at the top of each section. Tables stay.
720–919
Tablet narrow. Two-column → stacked. Charts shrink to 200pt tall. Topnav still full-width.
≤719
Phone. Tables → stacked cards. Topnav → hamburger (opens a slide-over drawer) + brand + search.
≤375
Phone min. Hit targets ≥ 44pt enforced. Sticky vote CTA on proposal detail. Notch + safe-area aware.
All
AI panels keep their hatched background and △ AI mark at every breakpoint — never collapse to plain text.
