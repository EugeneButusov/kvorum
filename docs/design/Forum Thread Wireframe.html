Following up on last month's risk thread, here is the proposal to migrate cUSDC to IRM v3. Curve parameters attached. Snapshot of expected APY range under historical utilization in the linked spreadsheet.

Three changes from v2: kink at 80% (was 90%), base 0% (was 0.5%), multiplier 4.5% (was 4%). Rationale: shifts more rate response into the middle band, reduces idle yield on protocol-owned reserves.

Voting opens Wed. Calldata will be posted in this thread before submission.
